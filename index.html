Budget Portfolio

One of 3 supply code sample calculations out of 15 total that I developed as part of a group budget proposal assignment. Final product/s was developed using Microsoft Word, while collaboration was facilitated through Google Docs. Additionally, Google Sheets was utilized to accurately calculate and track funds, and to create pie charts illustrating the differences between fiscal years (“Financial Concepts for Healthcare Managers”, UW – Winter 2023).


Tableau Dashboard

Dashboard created as a self-learning exploratory project displaying key metrics and trends related to Seattle's Airbnb market for 2023. Dashboard allows you to gain insights into the market and its effect on the city's housing market. Data provided by Inside Airbnb (http://insideairbnb.com/) to uncover Airbnb’s impact on residential communities.


R Plot

An R script and Plot created from data on colorectal screening status. Data was analyzed on excel, and imported into RStudio to create a clustered bar chart that visually presented the data. Plot visually shows the number of patients who have Due, Not Due, or Overdue status in their EHR, by determined screening frequency. Created using EHR Go, Excel, and RStudio. (“Research Design and Statistics for HIHIM”, UW - Winter 2023).


Statement of Work

Infographic
